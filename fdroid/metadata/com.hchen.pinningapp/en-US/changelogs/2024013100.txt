### PinningApp v1.0

### 中文
- ### 固定应用模块首发！
- 模块效果：
- 当进入固定模式时，手机将只显示被固定App的界面，系统返回等手势将失效。
- ~~类似IOS的引导式访问功能~~
- ### 模块功能：
- 手机：
- 固定应用、拒绝返回手势、拒绝呼出侧边栏（可选）、解除固定时锁屏（可选）等。
- Pad：
- 固定应用、拒绝返回手势、拒绝呼出侧边栏（可选）、解除固定时锁屏（可选）、
- 拒绝呼出Dock栏、拒绝响应小窗手势、隐藏小白条 等。
- #### 详细请参考README.md

### English
- ### First Release of the Pinning App Module!
- Module Effects:
- When entering pinning mode, the phone will only display the interface of the pinning app, and system gestures such as back will be disabled.
- ~~Similar to IOS's guided access feature.~~
- ### Module Features:
- For Phones:
- Pinning App, reject back gestures, reject pop-up sidebar (optional), lock screen on exit (optional), etc.
- For Pads:
- Pinning App, reject back gestures, reject pop-up sidebar (optional), lock screen on exit (optional),
- Reject call-out of Dock bar, reject response to small window gestures, hide the small white bar, etc.
- #### For details, please refer to README.md.
