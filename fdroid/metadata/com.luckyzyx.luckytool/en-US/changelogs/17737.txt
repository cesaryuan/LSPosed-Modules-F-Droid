## Notes

ZH
ColorOS15必须优化所有的作用域

首先使用潮汐引擎里的应用优化，然后使用LSPosed管理器对模块的全部作用域进行长按优化，最后重启系统

并且系统更新后必须重新进行此操作

EN
ColorOS15 must optimize all scopes

First use the application optimization in the Tide Engine, then use the LSPosed manager to long-press and optimize all scopes of the module, and finally restart the system

And this operation must be repeated after the system is updated
