# 📦 Release: Version 0.2.1  
🎉 InstaEclipse Update 🎉  

We’re excited to bring you Version 0.2.1 of InstaEclipse! 🚀  

---

## 🆕 What's New?  

### 👻 **Ghost Mode Enhancements**  
- Added **Hide Typing Status**: Stay completely invisible while replying to DMs.

### 🧘 **Distraction-Free Mode Enhancements**  
- Added **Hide Comments**: Remove comments from posts for an even cleaner experience.

### 🌍 **Improved App Translation Compatibility**  
- Full support for app translations by moving to `@string` resources for better adaptability and flexibility.

## 📌 Important Notes  

- **Compatible Instagram Version**: The module currently supports only Instagram versions from [APKMirror](https://www.apkmirror.com/apk/instagram/instagram-instagram/). Avoid using the Google Play version as it may not function correctly.  
- **LSPosed Compatibility**: For optimal performance, ensure you are using the latest LSPosed fork by [JingMatrix](https://github.com/JingMatrix/LSPosed).  
- **Feature Activation**:  
  - **Ghost Mode**: Works seamlessly after enabling via the module.  
  - **Distraction-Free Mode**: Force stop Instagram and clear its cache to apply changes properly.  
- **Developer Options**: Ensure you’re signed in before enabling and follow the guide for proper setup.
