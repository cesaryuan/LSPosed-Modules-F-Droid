# 📦 Release: Version 0.3  
🎉 InstaEclipse Update 🎉  

We’re thrilled to announce Version 0.3 of InstaEclipse! 🚀  

---

## 🆕 What's New?  

### 🔧 **New Features**  
- **Disable Auto Story Flipping**: Gives you control over story navigation.  
- **Disable Auto Play Videos**: Prevent videos from auto-playing for a smoother browsing experience.  

### 🛠️ **Improvements**  
- **DexKit Integration**: Integrated DexKit to enhance dynamic analysis and improve compatibility with Instagram updates.  
- **UI Enhancements**: Improved user interface for a more seamless experience.  
- **Bug Fix**: Resolved an issue with Developer Options that caused unexpected behavior.  

---

## 📌 Important Notes  

- **Compatible Instagram Version**: Ensure you install Instagram via [APKMirror](https://www.apkmirror.com/apk/instagram/instagram-instagram/). The Google Play version may not be fully supported.  
- **LSPosed Compatibility**: Use the latest LSPosed fork by [JingMatrix](https://github.com/JingMatrix/LSPosed) for the best experience.
