# 📦 Release: Version 0.1
🎉 **InstaEclipse Early Access Release** 🎉

This is the first version of InstaEclipse, designed to enhance your Instagram experience. 🚀  

## **Features**  
- 🎛️ **Developer Options**: Unlock hidden features within Instagram.  
- 👻 **Ghost Mode**: Stay invisible while viewing stories, lives, or DMs.  
- 🧘 **Distraction-Free Mode**: Hide stories, reels, and explore feed.  
- 🚫 **Ad-Free Experience**: Removes most intrusive ads from your feed.  
  - **Note**: Disabling ads is not fully implemented yet; some ads may still appear.

## **Important Notes**  
- 🛠️ **Distraction-Free Mode** requires clearing Instagram's cache after enabling.  
- 🚧 **Developer Options** work best with **Alpha** or **Beta** versions of Instagram.
