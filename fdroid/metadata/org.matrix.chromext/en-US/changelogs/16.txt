Hello, everyone, this is once again a minor update, which mainly contains various fixes to support updated browsers.
For Android 15 users, please consider use my forks of [LSPosed](https://github.com/JingMatrix/LSPosed) and [LSPatch](https://github.com/JingMatrix/LSPatch) since the official developments has stopped.

3. Add workarounds for Edge browsers, see [Issues 156](https://github.com/JingMatrix/ChromeXt/issues/156);
4. Support Qihoo and Chromium browsers;
